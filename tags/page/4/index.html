For elderly or disabled individuals, managing appointments can be a challenging task. Text Message Marketing Automation

You can do a lot with SMS Marketing. By setting up highly targeted campaigns to customers that haven't heard from you (or is simply ignoring your emails along with all the other promotional emails they get in their inbox) you can grow your business and consistently deliver value to your customers so they rave about your services to their friends and family.

Text Message Marketing Best Practices

The limitations of SMS is a double edged sword. You only have so many characters to share your message but that limitation can actually be a knock-out punch if crafted carefully. The cost of sending SMS combined with the character limitations forces us to carefully manage who we send our messages to, when we send our messages, and what we say in our messages.

Text Message VS Email Marketing

People are inundated with emails and gone are the days of 90% open rates. Or are they?

Text messaging still boasts a 90% open rate and the few saavy businesses that are utilizing them well are getting mutliples on their ROI. Compared to marketing channels like facebook and adwords where the cost of attention has gotten a lot more expensive than they used to be, utlilizing SMS marketing is becoming a more reliable investment to boost revenue.

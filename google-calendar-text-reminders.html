Google Calendar Text Reminders

Google Calendars has become so common in our everyday lives that we expect all of our devices and software tools to integrate seamlessly with the Google Suite of products. Unfortunately, the default reminder system provided by Google is limited to emails we ignore. In this tutorial, we will show you how to easily set custom schedules for reminders as sms text messages.


Connecting With Google Calendar

The easiest way to get started is to import your events from Google Calendar to a system that has SMS text capabilities.

Rezque Appointment Reminders synchronizes with Google Calendar to import all of your events. Rezque will automatically import emails, phone numbers, and any custom field you define.

For custom fields, all you need to do is separate the key from the value with a colon and you can use it as a custom field in your message templates.


Creating Message Templates

You can set custom schedules for your templates by defining when in advance to send the message, what time window to allow messages to be sent, and based on what conditions to send the message. Having granular control over how and when your messages are sent, your customers will be delighted to hear from you.

Visualizing Your Appointments

You have the option of having your customer's confirm appointmnets via sms text message by replaying with '1' or via email with the optional confirmation link provided in your message templates.

Once your contact has been reminded, your event title is amended with [R] so that you can see which appointments have been reminded. And when your contact has confirmed, your event title will be amended with [C] to reflect the confirmation so you have confidence that the likelihood of a no-show is low.

And since two-way synchronization is the set by default, your Google Calendar will be update instantly so that you know as soon as your contact is reminded or confirmed.


SMS Responses

When a reminded appointment responds to your SMS text message that isn't a confirmation, a email notification with your contact's information and response so that you can take note of any special requests made by your customer.

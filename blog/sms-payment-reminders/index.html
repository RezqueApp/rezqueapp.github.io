SMS Payment Reminders

It's hard to keep track of all the payments that need to made. It's even more difficult to track the payments that haven't been made by a business. From lawyers to accountants, the need to collect payments on a timely business is crucial to keeping the lights on and making sure that payroll is met. Yet most businesses still miss receiving payments because they simply forget to remind their clients.

Our customers use Rezque to schedule automated text message reminders to all of their customers that are on a regular schedule of payments. They even use calendars that they're familiar with like Google Calendars and set up recurring schedules depending on the payment terms and Rezque syncs the events and schedules sms text messages based on templates that they create.

Whether you collect payments for rent, a subscription service, a credit card company, or event a utility company, automated sms reminders ensures that your company gets paid and that you're not left with angry customers complaining about late fees or even worse, canceled subscriptions and unpaid bills.
